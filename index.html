🔮 Wisdom of the Realm

Answers to the questions every traveler asks

What is a sign-up bonus?

It is a reward a bank pays you for opening an account or card. You usually must meet a simple condition first, like making a purchase or a deposit.

Will applying hurt my credit score?

Credit cards often need a "hard inquiry," which may lower your score a little for a short time. Many checking accounts use only a "soft pull," which does not affect your score. Apply only for what you truly need.

What is a direct deposit?

It is money sent straight into your account, such as a paycheck or government benefits. Many bonuses require one. Transfers from PayPal or Venmo usually do not count.

How long until I get my reward?

It varies. Some arrive in a few days. Others take a few weeks after you meet all the terms. Always read the fine print of each offer.

Are these accounts safe?

The banks and partners listed here use FDIC-insured deposit accounts. Still, always confirm the latest details on each provider's official website before you join.

How do referral links work?

When you join through my link, the company often rewards both of us. You get your bonus, and I get a small thank-you. It costs you nothing extra.

📖 The Coin-Keeper's Glossary

Common terms, explained in plain words

APR
The yearly interest rate on money you borrow. A 0% intro APR means no interest for a set time.
APY
The yearly rate your savings earn. A higher APY means more growth on your money.
Cash Back
A small share of your spending returned to you as money.
Credit Score
A number that shows how trustworthy you are with borrowing. Higher is better.
Direct Deposit
Money paid straight into your account, like a paycheck.
FDIC Insured
Your deposits are protected by the U.S. government, up to legal limits.
Hard vs. Soft Inquiry
A hard inquiry can lower your score a little. A soft inquiry does not affect it.
Statement Credit
A reward added to your account that lowers your balance.
Welcome / Sign-Up Bonus
The reward you earn for joining and meeting simple terms.
